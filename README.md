# SheAffirm - Daily Positive Affirmations

## Empowering Women Through Positive Self-Talk

SheAffirm is a simple yet powerful web application designed to boost confidence and positivity for women through daily affirmations. In a world that often challenges women's self-worth, SheAffirm provides a peaceful digital space for reflection, encouragement, and self-love.

## The Power of Affirmations

Positive affirmations are more than just feel-good phrases—they're a scientifically-backed tool for rewiring our thought patterns and building self-confidence. SheAffirm makes this practice accessible, beautiful, and effortless.

## Features

### 🌸 Daily Affirmations
Open the app to receive a carefully crafted positive affirmation designed to uplift and inspire. Each message focuses on strength, self-worth, and personal growth.

### 🔄 Generate New Affirmations
Not feeling today's message? Simply click "New Affirmation" to receive another empowering statement. With 20 unique affirmations, there's always something that resonates.

### ⭐ Save Your Favorites
Found an affirmation that speaks to your soul? Save it to your favorites collection. Your saved affirmations persist across sessions, creating a personal library of encouragement you can revisit anytime.

### 🎨 Calming Design
The interface features soft lavender, gentle pink, and warm coral tones that create a soothing, welcoming atmosphere. Every element is designed to feel peaceful and positive.

## Technology

SheAffirm is built with simplicity and accessibility in mind:

- **HTML5** - Semantic, accessible structure
- **CSS3** - Beautiful gradients, smooth animations, responsive design
- **Vanilla JavaScript** - No frameworks, no dependencies, just pure functionality
- **LocalStorage** - Your favorites are saved locally in your browser

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Read your daily affirmation
3. Click "New Affirmation" to see more messages
4. Click "Save to Favorites" to keep affirmations you love
5. View and manage your favorites in the section below

## Why SheAffirm?

In just a few seconds each day, SheAffirm can help you:
- Start your morning with positivity
- Boost confidence before important moments
- Remind yourself of your worth during challenging times
- Build a habit of positive self-talk
- Create a personal collection of empowering messages

## Design Philosophy

SheAffirm embraces minimalism and intentionality. There are no distractions, no ads, no tracking—just you and your affirmations. The calming color palette and gentle animations create a moment of peace in your busy day.

## Browser Compatibility

SheAffirm works on all modern browsers:
- Chrome
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

The responsive design ensures a beautiful experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

## Privacy

Your data never leaves your device. All favorites are stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. No servers, no tracking, no data collection.
